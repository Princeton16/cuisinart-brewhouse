What changed

Calmer surfaces. Same magazine bones.

Pulled the SVG noise overlay off the body. Replaced every 6/8 px hard offset ink shadow with a soft two-layer drop shadow. Un-rotated the cover ribbon, the giveaway sticker, and the tomato/teal headline blocks. Dropped 2 px ink borders down to 1 px hairlines on chips, tiles, and dividers. Net effect: the photography reads first, the type reads second, and the stickerish texture stops competing.
